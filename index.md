Welcome to the official website of the 2nd Latin American Music Information
Retrieval (LAMIR) Workshop! LAMIR is a satellite event of the ISMIR 2026
conference. It provides opportunities for
local students and researchers to cultivate the Latin American community within
the [International Society for Music Information Retrieval](https://ismir.net/) 
(ISMIR) and the Artificial Intelligence (AI) communities.

The workshop will take place on December 14 – 17, 2026, at the Universidade
Federal de Minas Gerais (UFMG) in Belo Horizonte, Brazil.

Please see the [Call for Papers](call4papers) page for more information.

Similar to LAMIR 2024, we plan on offering financial support in the form of travel
awards to local Latin American students. Please check back soon.

Please feel free to contact us with any questions or concerns related to the 
workshop at
<a href="mailto:lamir.workshop@gmail.com">lamir.workshop@gmail.com</a>.
