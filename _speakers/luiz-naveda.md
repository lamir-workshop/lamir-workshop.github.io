Luiz Naveda is an artist, musician, researcher, composer, multimedia project designer, and educator whose work bridges the fields of music, dance, movement, and technology. He is a professor and researcher at the State University of Minas Gerais (UEMG), where he leads Corpuslab, a research group dedicated to the study of music, dance, human movement, and applied technologies. He coordinates a range of interdisciplinary projects that bring together music, dance, art, and technology in collaboration with national and international research networks. In addition, he supervises graduate research across multiple levels, fostering innovative work at the intersection of science and the arts.
